My name is Erica Remboske. I am hoping to one day work in the arts & entertainment field as I love to write more with music reviews, concert reviews, and latest news that happens with celebrities. My lifelong dream is to become a writer for J-14, Seventeen or any other pop culture news outlet. I am also open to doing an internship that will help me gain experience before I jump right in. I am willing to learn anything and everyone even if it is not in my career field of choice. I am a hardworking individual and I love to express myself within my writing. As you look through my portfolio, I hope you see that this shines through my work.


I have written for multiple publications such as The Signal (the college paper), Next Wave Magazine, and Sweety High. I have learned more about editing and writing for each audience. This creates diverse opportunities that I can take.
